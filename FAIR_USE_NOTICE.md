The **MotionSites Prompts Collection** contains educational materials, structural blueprints, and conceptual prompts designed to explore the capabilities of AI in modern web design.

## 17 U.S.C. § 107 (Fair Use)
This repository may contain concepts, structural layouts, or design patterns that are common in the web development industry. This material is distributed without profit for research and educational purposes. 

Under **17 U.S.C. § 107** of the US Copyright Law, the fair use of a copyrighted work for purposes such as criticism, comment, news reporting, teaching, scholarship, or research, is not an infringement of copyright. 

### Transformative Nature
The prompts provided in this repository are **highly transformative**. They do not copy source code or duplicate visual assets. Instead, they provide textual, structural instructions for AI models to generate *new, original, and independent* code and layouts based on general industry trends (e.g., Glassmorphism, Bento grids, dark mode UI).

### Effect on the Market
The distribution of these prompts does not negatively impact the potential market for or value of any third-party copyrighted works. This repository is an educational tool for developers and designers to learn prompt engineering.

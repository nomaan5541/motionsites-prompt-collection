# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Respectful communication
- Accepting constructive feedback
- Being inclusive and welcoming

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment or discrimination
- Trolling, insulting comments
- Publishing private information

## Enforcement
Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards.

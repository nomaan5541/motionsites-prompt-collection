# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

**Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:**
- Respectful and inclusive communication
- Accepting constructive feedback gracefully
- Being welcoming to newcomers
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members

**Examples of unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation in any form
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, or personal attacks
- Publishing private information without permission
- Submitting copyrighted or trademarked content without authorization
- Any conduct that could be reasonably considered inappropriate

## Content Standards

In addition to behavioral standards, all contributions must:
- Be **original work** or properly attributed
- **NOT** include copyrighted material, trademarked brands, or proprietary content
- **NOT** clone or reproduce real company designs, logos, or brand identities
- Respect intellectual property rights at all times

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior and will take appropriate corrective action in response to any instances of unacceptable behavior.

## Reporting

If you experience unacceptable behavior, please report it by opening a GitHub issue with **[COC]** in the title.
